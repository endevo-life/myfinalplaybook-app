## 1. Overview
MyFinalPlaybook is a free educational app that helps adults assess their
"legacy readiness" across four areas (Digital, Legal, Financial, Physical) and
follow a 7-day action plan. This policy explains what information the App
collects, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have.

This App is **not** intended for children under 13 (or under 16 where required),
and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

> ⚖️ **Legal note:** The App provides educational content only and is **not**
> legal, financial, tax, or medical advice. Confirm appropriate disclaimers with
> counsel.

## 2. Information we collect

### a) Information you provide
- **First name** — to personalize your plan and report.
- **Email address** — to send your results and (if you opt in) reminders/tips.
- **Marketing consent** — whether you toggled on daily reminders and tips.

### b) Information generated by using the App
- **Assessment responses and results** — your answers to the 12 questions and
  the resulting scores, readiness band, and weakest/second-weakest domains.
- **Platform** — whether you use the iOS or Android version.
- **Usage/analytics events** — e.g. when you start or complete the assessment,
  capture your email, or open a daily action. `[CONFIRM: analytics provider —

### c) Information stored only on your device
